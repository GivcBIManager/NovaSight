Möbius Strip Topology

✓ Real Crossing One part of the band visibly passes OVER the other — the defining visual of a Möbius strip
✓ Single Band One continuous ribbon forming a closed loop with consistent width throughout
✓ 180° Half-Twist The band twists once — outer edge becomes inner edge, front face becomes back face
✓ Over/Under Depth Back-to-front rendering creates real occlusion — the crossing isn't faked with outlines
✓ Continuous Color Shift Teal (front face) → deep blue (back face) — shading rotates through the twist
✓ One-Sided Surface After the twist, the "back" IS the "front" — Möbius topology means one continuous surface
